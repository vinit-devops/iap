# M24.4 Coverage-Tiling Audit — every target service in exactly one bucket

**Date:** 2026-07-18 · **Milestone:** M24.4 (ROADMAP-V4 closeout) ·
**Purpose:** assert that each of the 50 target AWS services lands in **exactly one** of four
buckets — **live-proven**, **mock-only** (handler ships, live run deferred at a gate),
**deferred-with-reason** (no handler, deferred at a recorded gate), or **cut-at-a-gate** — and
that no service in the roadmap scope has _neither a handler nor a recorded deferral_.

**Method.** Cross-checked three ground-truth sources:

1. `packages/deploy-aws/src/registry.ts` → **68** registered handlers (`SUPPORTED_TARGET_TYPES`);
   ground truth for which handlers ship executable.
2. `docs/reports/*-live-run-evidence.md` (49 live-run docs) + the 3 deferral records
   (Timestream, MSK, and the M24.3 stretch wave); ground truth for what was live-proven vs deferred.
3. `ROADMAP-V4.yml` phases 21–24 (milestone statuses + evidence lines).
