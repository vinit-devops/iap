## Design rationale

Blanket recursive materialization would have _synthesized_ semantics (an absent `healthCheck` does not mean "health-check with default interval") and even manufactured IAP104 violations (`deadLetter.maxReceives` materialized beside `enabled: false`). The presence-semantic carve-out plus conditional defaults keep ┬¦2.7's "defaults are invisible in canonical form" promise everywhere it is semantically true, and nowhere it is false.
